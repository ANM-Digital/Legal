About This App

The 99 Names of Allah (Esmaul Husna) app is designed to help Muslims learn, understand, and reflect upon the beautiful names of Allah. Each name represents an attribute of Allah and carries deep spiritual meaning.

This application provides a simple and accessible way to explore the 99 Names through Arabic text, transliteration, and explanations. The goal is to make learning and remembrance easier for users around the world.

Features

• Complete list of the 99 Names of Allah
• Arabic text with transliteration
• Meanings and explanations
• Audio pronunciation
• Clean and simple interface

Educational Purpose

This app is intended for educational and spiritual purposes only. The content is provided to help users learn about the names and attributes of Allah and to encourage remembrance (dhikr).
