About This App

The 99 Names of Allah (Esmaul Husna) app is designed to help users learn, understand, and reflect upon the beautiful names of Allah. Each name represents an important attribute of Allah and holds deep spiritual meaning in Islam.

This application provides a simple and modern way to explore the 99 Names through Arabic text, transliteration, meanings, and audio pronunciation.

Features

  • Complete list of the 99 Names of Allah
  • Arabic names with transliteration
  • English and Turkish meanings
  • Audio pronunciation
  • Simple and clean user interface

Purpose

This app is developed for educational and spiritual purposes. It aims to make it easier for users to learn and remember the beautiful names of Allah.

Developer

This application is developed and maintained by ANM Digital Labs, part of the ANM Consultancy ecosystem founded by Mutlu Akgün.

ANM Digital Labs focuses on building digital applications, educational tools, and scalable content platforms.
