About the 99 Names of Allah App

The 99 Names of Allah (Esmaul Husna) app is created to help users learn, understand, and reflect on the beautiful names of Allah. Each name represents one of the divine attributes of Allah and holds deep spiritual significance in Islamic tradition.

This application offers an easy and modern way to explore the 99 Names through Arabic text, transliteration, meanings, and audio pronunciation, making learning accessible to users around the world.

App Features

  • Complete collection of the 99 Names of Allah
  • Arabic text with accurate transliteration
  • English and Turkish meanings and explanations
  • Audio pronunciation for each name
  • Simple and clean user interface

Purpose of the App

The purpose of this application is educational and spiritual. It is designed to help users remember and reflect on the names and attributes of Allah while supporting learning and personal spiritual growth.
